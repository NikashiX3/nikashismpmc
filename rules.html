Follow the rules

List of all Nikashi Network and Nikashi Community rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Nikashi Network Minecraft Server

  1. Chat Abuse :
    It is prohibited to use offensive language, make inappropriate jokes or topics, spread misleading information, or spam in any form (including switching between chat instances), and promote other servers. Excessive use of caps lock or trolling in the chat is also not allowed. Swearing is allowed, but it must not be directed toward other players. Hate speech, slurs, and excessive toxicity are not tolerated under any circumstances.
  2. Threats :
    Threatening, doxing, DDOSing, harassing, or extorting other players is prohibited. Sharing personal information or sending harmful links is also not allowed.
  3. Unfair Advantage :
    Using unauthorized modifications, abusing bugs, exploiting, scripting, or evading punishments is not allowed. All bugs or exploits found must be reported to the admin immediately.
  4. Account Abuse :
    Selling, trading, giving, or sharing accounts between players is prohibited. Any form of account abuse will be strictly dealt with. Using accounts that do not belong to the player, such as "borrowed" accounts, is also forbidden.
  5. Game Trading :
    Exchanging in-game content, items, currency, services, or accounts through unauthorized third-party platforms or external game trading platforms is prohibited. This extends to, but is not limited to, activities such as organizing or participating in games of chance, including gambling or setting up a casino on the server.
  6. Inappropriate Content and Cosmetics :
    Using inappropriate cloaks, skins, usernames, item names, tags, pet names, Housing content, Guild content, or any other inappropriate content is prohibited. Usernames that advertise other servers or external sites are also not allowed.
  7. Gameplay Abuse :
    It is forbidden to use cloaks, skins, usernames, item names, tags, pet names, Housing content, Guild content, or other inappropriate content. Usernames that contain advertisements for other servers or external sites are also prohibited.
  8. Random Territory Claiming :
    It is forbidden to claim territories randomly without filling them with meaningful structures or buildings. Empty territory claims without purpose are not allowed (Including Main End Island).
  9. Claim Trial Dungeon :
    It is forbidden to claim any building or structure that contains a Trial Spawner. This means the area is open to the public and may not be claimed by anyone.
  10. Mob Farm Management :
    Mob Farms must have a proper management system, such as directing mob drops into storage like chests or barrels, to prevent mob drop items from piling up on the ground. Turn off the Mob Farm when not in use, such as by disabling the Mob Spawn flag in that Mob Farm.

Nikashi Community Discord Server

  1. Be Respectful :
    Treat all members with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and hate speech will not be tolerated.
  2. Use Channels Appropriately :
    Please use each channel according to its designated purpose. This helps keep the server organized and ensures that discussions stay relevant.
  3. No Spamming :
    Avoid spamming messages, emojis, or images. Keep the chat clean and relevant.
  4. No Self-Promotion :
    Do not promote your own content, servers, or social media without permission from the moderators.
  5. No NSFW Content :
    This server is family-friendly. Do not share any NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content.
  6. No Cheating or Hacking :
    Discussions or sharing of cheats, hacks, or exploits are strictly prohibited.
  7. Follow Discord’s Terms of Service :
    Ensure you comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  8. Respect Privacy :
    Do not record or screenshot conversations without consent.
  9. Listen to Moderators :
    Follow the instructions of moderators and admins. They are here to help maintain order and ensure everyone has a good time.
  10. Do not promote, coordinate, or engage in harassment :
    This includes sustained bullying, sexual harassment, ban or block evasion, or coordinating server joins for the purpose of harassing server members.
  11. Do not threaten to harm another individual or group of people :
    This includes direct, indirect, and suggestive threats.
  12. Do not share or threaten to share personally identifiable information (PII) :
    This includes providing services that facilitate doxxing.
  13. Do not use hate speech or engage in other hateful conduct :
    This includes the use of hate symbols, imagery, and claims that deny the history of mass human atrocities.
  14. Do not organize, promote, or support violent extremism :
    This includes coordinating violent acts, glorifying or promoting violence, and promoting conspiracy theories that could incite violence.

    - Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.

  5. Changes to This Privacy Policy :
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our server and website, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically.
  6. Contact Us :
    If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Contact Us.
